Who We Are

NICO Insurance is one of Malawi's leading insurance providers, offering a wide range of reliable insurance solutions for individuals, families, and businesses. The company provides motor, property, health, travel, life, and business insurance products designed to protect customers from financial risks and unexpected events.

As part of the NICO Group, NICO Insurance is committed to delivering trusted service, innovative products, and customer-focused solutions while contributing to Malawi's economic growth and helping clients achieve long-term financial security.
